# About EaseTrack

EaseTrack is a simple and intuitive health‑tracking app designed to help you monitor pain, symptoms, and vital signs. All data is stored **locally on your device**, giving you full control over your personal health information.

## Key Features
- Track pain levels and locations  
- Record vital signs (temperature, blood pressure, oxygen, sugar)  
- View charts and history  
- Export data as CSV or PDF  
- No accounts, no cloud, no tracking  

EaseTrack is built for privacy, simplicity, and personal health awareness.
