# Privacy Policy for EaseTrack
Last updated: April 2026

## 1. Introduction
EaseTrack is designed to help users track pain, symptoms, and health‑related information. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and how it is stored.

## 2. Information We Collect
All information you enter into EaseTrack is optional and provided voluntarily by you. The app may store the following data if you choose to enter it:

- Personal details (name, age, weight, height, gender)  
- Pain records (location, date, time, pain level)  
- Vital signs (temperature, blood pressure, oxygen level, sugar level)

EaseTrack does **not** automatically collect any personal data.

## 3. How Your Data Is Used
Your data is used only inside the app to:
- Track symptoms over time  
- Display charts and summaries  
- Help you monitor your health patterns  

We do **not** use your data for analytics, advertising, or any external processing.

## 4. Data Storage and Security
All data is stored **locally on your device**.  
No information is uploaded, transmitted, or backed up to any server by the app.  
Your data is protected by your device’s built‑in security features.

## 5. Data Sharing
We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion
Your data remains on your device until you choose to delete it. You can delete all stored data at any time by:
- Using the **Delete All Data** button inside the app, or  
- Uninstalling the app (this removes all internal app data)

If you exported files (CSV or PDF), they remain in your device’s Downloads folder and must be deleted manually if you no longer need them.

## 7. Data Export
If you export your data:
- Files are saved to your device’s Downloads folder  
- Exported files are not encrypted once outside the app  
- You are responsible for storing or deleting exported files safely  

## 8. Children’s Privacy
EaseTrack is not intended for children under 13.  
We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 9. Medical Disclaimer
EaseTrack does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.  
Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns.
